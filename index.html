02 / About

Senior retail and strategy executive with 20+ years of experience leading and scaling multi-format retail and consumer business across Myanmar (SEA) and the United Kingdom. Extensive background in group strategy, omnichannel retail, and business incubation, with a consistent record of strengthening organizational capability and operational discipline in both developed and emerging markets.

Experienced in corporate governance, digital transformation, and organizational restructuring, with hands-on leadership through periods of rapid growth, market volatility, and operational complexity. Brings a pragmatic, execution-focused approach, supported by cross-cultural leadership experience and strong stakeholder engagement across boards, regulators, and industry partners.

Currently serves as Vice President of the Myanmar Retailers Association (MMRA), contributing to industry coordination, standards development, and sector-level dialogue.
